Key Staff

Ben Ghorbanpour, Ph.D., P.E.

Founder & Principal Geotechnical Engineer

Dr. Ben Ghorbanpour is the founder and principal geotechnical engineer at ABG Engineering. With over 15 years of experience, he brings extensive expertise in geotechnical, foundation, and seismic engineering across a broad range of sectors. Dr. Ghorbanpour holds a PhD in Geotechnical Engineering and has led numerous complex projects for clients in infrastructure, energy, mining, commercial, industrial, and residential development.

Erika Larson, Ph.D.

Marketing & Sales Manager

Dr. Erika Larson is our Marketing and Sales Manager and the friendly face behind many of your first interactions with our team. She oversees our marketing efforts and works closely with clients to understand their needs and connect them with the right solutions. With a strong understanding of the geotechnical industry and a genuine passion for building relationships, Erika helps make the process efficient from start to finish.
