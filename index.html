What we do

Our expertise in geotechnical and earthquake engineering spans diverse sectors—including commercial and industrial developments, transportation networks, energy facilities, and critical civil infrastructure. Below is a summary of the services we provide.

Geotechnical & Foundation Engineering

Foundation engineering services, slope stability evaluations and mitigation, shorings, retaining walls, and ground improvement tailored to each project’s unique conditions is our expertise.

Geotechnical Earthquake Engineering

We deliver advanced geotechnical earthquake engineering services to support your geotechnical and structural projects with reliable, performance-based solutions.

Seismic Hazard Assessment

We perform site-specific seismic hazard analyses (SHA) and develop acceleration time histories tailored to each project’s unique conditions and seismic design requirements.

Numerical Modeling

We use advanced numerical modeling approaches, such as finite element analysis, to solve complex geotechnical engineering challenges from soil–structure interaction to dynamic response analysis.

Geotechnical Explorations

We have extensive experience conducting geotechnical site explorations using a wide range of drilling techniques and in-situ testing methods.

Expert Witness and Litigation Support

With our deep technical expertise, we provide reliable expert witness and litigation support services.
